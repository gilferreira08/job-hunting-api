# SCORE_RULES.md

# Purpose

This file defines the scoring methodology for evaluating job opportunities in the Job Hunt Dashboard.

The scoring system is designed for a senior finance profile focused on:
- Treasury
- Funding
- Liquidity
- Hedging
- Project Finance
- Debt Structuring
- Treasury Transformation
- Strategic Finance

The objective is NOT to rank jobs generically.

The objective IS to measure how strongly each opportunity matches the candidate’s strategic positioning and probability of interview success.

---

# Global Scoring Model

Every job receives a score from 0 to 100.

The overall score is calculated using the following weights:

| Category | Weight |
|---|---|
| Treasury / Hedging Relevance | 30% |
| Project Finance Exposure | 20% |
| Debt / Funding / Refinancing | 15% |
| Seniority Match | 15% |
| Tools & Systems | 10% |
| Location Fit | 10% |

Total = 100%

---

# Formula

```text
Overall Score =
(TreasuryHedgingScore * 0.30)
+ (ProjectFinanceScore * 0.20)
+ (DebtFundingScore * 0.15)
+ (SeniorityScore * 0.15)
+ (ToolsSystemsScore * 0.10)
+ (LocationScore * 0.10)
```

Each sub-score must be between 0 and 100.

# Category 1 — Treasury / Hedging Relevance

## Weight
30%

## Measures
How strongly the job aligns with:
- Treasury Front Office
- Corporate Treasury
- Liquidity Management
- Cash Forecasting
- FX Hedging
- Interest Rate Hedging
- Financial Risk Management
- Treasury Advisory
- Treasury Governance
- Treasury Transformation

## Scoring Guide

### 90–100
Role is directly centered on treasury, liquidity, hedging, funding, or treasury front office.

Examples:
- Treasury Manager
- Treasury Front Office Manager
- Head of Treasury
- Liquidity Manager
- Treasury Risk Manager
- Treasury Advisory Manager

### 75–89
Role has strong treasury exposure but also includes other finance responsibilities.

Examples:
- Strategic Treasury
- Treasury Transformation
- Treasury Systems
- Corporate Finance with treasury ownership

### 50–74
Role has partial treasury relevance but treasury is not central.

Examples:
- Finance Transformation
- FP&A with cash/liquidity focus
- Risk role with treasury exposure

### 25–49
Treasury is peripheral.

### 0–24
No treasury or hedging relevance.

# Recommendation Thresholds

## Apply Now
Overall score >= 80
AND role is verified active
AND application link works
AND role is not duplicate/applied/excluded.

## Consider
Overall score between 70 and 79
OR strategic value exists but meaningful gaps remain.

## Skip
Overall score < 70
OR role is junior/accounting/audit/back-office/tax-heavy/out of scope
OR application link is invalid
OR role is closed.

---

# Hard Exclusion Rules

Automatically exclude:
- Junior roles
- Internships
- Graduate programs
- Accounting-only roles
- Audit-only roles
- AP / AR roles
- Tax-heavy treasury roles
- German-only roles
- Compliance-only risk roles
- Back-office-only roles
- Low analytical finance operations
- Generic administrative finance
- Invalid application links
- Closed postings
- Duplicate postings
- Already applied roles

---

# Verification Rules

A role MUST NOT be recommended unless:
1. The application link works.
2. The job page opens correctly.
3. The role title is visible.
4. The Apply button exists.
5. The role is active/open.
6. The role is strategically aligned.
7. The role is not duplicate.
8. The role is not already applied.
9. The role is not excluded/out of scope.
10. The role was published recently OR clearly marked active.

---

# Verification Penalty Rules

## Invalid Application Link
Set:
- Recommendation = Skip
- Status = Link Invalid

Set:
- Recommendation = Skip

## Duplicate Role
Set:
- Recommendation = Skip
- Status = Duplicate

## Already Applied
Set:
- Recommendation = Already Applied

## Out of Scope
Set:
- Recommendation = Skip

# Final Score Formula

Display:
1. Weighted Technical Score
2. Board Average Score
3. Final Score

Recommended Final Score formula:

```text
Final Score =
(Weighted Technical Score * 0.65)
+ (Board Average Score * 0.35)
```

---

# Priority Ranking

## Very High
Final score >= 88

## High
Final score 80–87

## Medium
Final score 70–79

## Low
Final score < 70

## Excluded
Closed, invalid, duplicate, already applied or out of scope.

# Important Principle

Do NOT inflate scores.

A role should only score above 85 if it strongly matches:
- Treasury
- Funding/Liquidity
- Hedging/Risk
- High Analytical Finance
- Strategic Positioning

A role should NOT be recommended only because it contains the word “Treasury”.

Quality and strategic fit matter more than title keywords.
